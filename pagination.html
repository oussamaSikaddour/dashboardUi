Pagination Component

The pagination component allows users to navigate between multiple pages of content, such as lists or tables. It provides navigation controls for first, previous, next, and last pages, along with a summary of the visible results.

  • .pagination__container — the outer wrapper that centers the pagination bar and applies background, border, and rounded corners.
  • .pagination — flex container handling alignment and spacing for the entire pagination area.
  • .pagination__info — displays the text summary such as “Showing 1 to 10 of 100 results”.
  • .pagination__links — contains navigation buttons for moving between pages.
  • .button:disabled — visually indicates that a navigation button (like “previous” on the first page) is inactive.

The pagination is responsive and automatically adapts its layout for small and large screens. Rounded corners are applied to the first and last buttons for a smooth, capsule-like design.

Example markup:



<nav class="pagination__container">

  <div role="navigation" aria-label="Pagination Navigation" class="pagination">

    <div class="pagination__content">

      <div class="pagination__info">

        <p>Showing <strong>1</strong> to <strong>2</strong> of <strong>3</strong> results</p>

      </div>

      <div class="pagination__links">

        <button type="button" class="button" disabled>

          <i class="fa-solid fa-angles-left"></i>

        </button>


        <button type="button" class="button" disabled>

          <i class="fa-solid fa-angle-left"></i>

        </button>


        <button type="button" class="button">

          <i class="fa-solid fa-angle-right"></i>

        </button>


        <button type="button" class="button">

          <i class="fa-solid fa-angles-right"></i>

        </button>

      </div>

    </div>

  </div>

</nav>
